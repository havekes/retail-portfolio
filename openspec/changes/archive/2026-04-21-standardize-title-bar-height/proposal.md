# Proposal: Standardize Title Bar Height

## Problem
The height of title bars (headers) varies across different pages depending on their content (e.g., whether they have subtitles, actions, or just a title). This leads to a jumpy and inconsistent user interface when navigating between views.

## Proposed Solution
Standardize the title bar height to a fixed 48px (`h-12`). This will be achieved by setting a 32px content area with 8px of padding on the top and bottom (`py-2`).

## Expected Impact
- Improved visual consistency across the application.
- More stable layout during navigation.
- Better alignment with design standards.
